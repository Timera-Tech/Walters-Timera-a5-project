# Walters-Timera-a5-project
Assignment 5. Finishing the Plinko template.

## How to play(Original)
Slide the mouse from left to right to decide where to drop the disk.
Left click to drop the disk. Left Click again to respawn.

## Planned addition / ending
The goal is to try and get 10000 points before the 10 second timer ends.
Once the timer ends and you have met the objective, you won.
If the timer ends and you have not met the objective, you lost.
